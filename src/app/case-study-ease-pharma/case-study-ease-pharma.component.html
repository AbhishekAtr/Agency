Ease Pharma Labs Case Study

Business Objective

Our Main objective was to develop an application to test different products for end user. We developed a process that will help manufacturers save their time by providing online services.

Business Challenges

Traffic Handling

Our challenge was to control number of visitors to the website and to gain control over the orders and type of visitors. Our team worked on website traffic management which helped us to preserve satisfactory performance

Boost User Experience

When it comes to site's performance, page speed is of the utmost importance. We understood how this metric works and worked to boost search engine rankings and enhance user experience on the website

Bulk Upload of Products

One of the hardest challenge for us was to upload multiple products. With the help of CSV file, we were able to overcome this challenge and import each and every product in bulk successfully through excel.

Business Solution

To build a successful Ease Pharma Labs software, our team worked together and took responsibility to remove each and every issue permanently. From gathering requirements to deployment of project our team co-ordinated with clients to reduce any type of miscommunication.
